Footloose

The Dance Event

Dancing is an effective and efficient shortcut to divinity and pleasure. It is the conversation between the body and soul. It certainly can be thought of as the most expressive and emotional form of art in nature. The charisma associated with this pristine form of art is so huge that IIIT Allahabad organized a mega dance event, Footloose, during Effervescence which promises to outpour the hearts desires with the beats of music.Learn More


EVENT GUIDLINES

  1. Team size 6 to 40

  2. Minimum 4 members must be present on the stage during the performance

  3. Participant must bring their music in pen drive which must be 6-10 minutes long

  4. There's only one round

  5. Performers have to clear the stage after their performance otherwise they will be penalized heavily by the organizers. 20% mars will be deducted if the performance exceeds by 10 sec. if the performance increases further 20sec then it will lead to disqualification.

JUDGING CRIETERIA

  1. Synchronisation,choreography,expression

  2. Energy level,stage utilization,costumes

  3. Overall impact,innovation,presentation,crowd appeal

GENERAL RULES

  1. Participants must get their songs music on CD and pen-drive. itis advised that they have backup in CD. Team Effervescence will not be responsible for this.

  2. Live music is not allowed

  3. The participating teams are requested to ensure that their finals should not be repeated and the performance is in keeping with the dignity of the fest.

  4. In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided , the participants will be allowed to repeat the performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the participant wishes, with the consent of the judge.

  5. Accessories, costumes, props will have to be arranged bt the participants

  6. The decision of the Judges and the organizing team will be final and binding.
